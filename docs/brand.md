# Brand & Content Specifications

## Author Persona

* **Pen Name:** Corven Locke
* **Tagline:** Queer love, impossible odds, and a dark edge. HEA or bust.
* **Bio Context for "About" Page:** Corven is a Berlin-based molecular biologist turned storyteller and indie author. They craft queer speculative fiction focusing on intricate worldbuilding, varied relationship dynamics (MM, FF, MMF, and beyond), and intense emotional stakes.

## Lead Title (The Prototype Hero Book)

* **Status:** Upcoming Release (Q2 2026)
* **Format:** Standalone Paranormal Gothic Romance
* **Working Title:** *Our Monstrous Lies*
* **Dynamics:** MM
* **Aesthetic/Vibe:** Fog on the moors, creeping dread, isolated manor, toxic-to-genuine regression.
* **Blurb/Summary Data:** Jem, aged out of foster care and drowning in debt to a local crime boss, takes a live-in PA job for Rowan, a wealthy man dying in an isolated manor. As the house's oppressive atmosphere escalates, Jem discovers Rowan is the victim of a family curse turning him into a monstrous vessel. What starts as a toxic physical arrangement masking genuine intimacy turns deadly when Jem learns Rowan plans to use him as a ritual sacrifice to break the curse. But when creditors come to collect Jem's life, both men secretly decide to sacrifice themselves to save the other.
* **Key Tropes:** Forced Proximity, Class Difference, Monster Romance, Hurt/Comfort, Touch-Starved, Defying Fate.
